Satellite Jump was developed by the team "Conglomerate Squad" for the Australian STEM Video Game Challenge 2022 Year 9-12: Playable game in Godot category.

About the game

In Satellite Jump you traverse through a network of satellites to deliver a message to the target satellite.

You'll have to find creative ways to traverse around planets and moons, and through space junk and portals using a limited number of moves (tutorials are provided). Complete all 30 levels to finally deliver the message to Earth.
